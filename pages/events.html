Upcoming Events

Easter Workshop (6th April)

Step into a vibrant celebration of Slovak Easter traditions! Enjoy a hands-on workshop where you'll decorate eggs using time-honored techniques, craft festive decorations, and learn about unique cultural customs. Live music, delicious Slovak treats, and a friendly social atmosphere ensure there's something for everyone. Whether you're coming with family, friends, or solo, you'll find plenty to inspire and connect you with our community.

Kids Disco 2025 (12th July)

Celebrate the end of the school year at our high-energy Kids Disco! Enjoy a lively dance floor set to a DJ’s beats, join in fun games, and take part in a special "Vysvedčenie" ceremony where children receive certificates and prizes for their achievements. This event is the perfect blend of excitement, community spirit, and celebration—ideal for families and friends looking to kick off summer in style. Stay tuned for more updates on our social media!
